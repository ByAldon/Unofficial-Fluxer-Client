Experience Fluxer on your Desktop.

A fast, unofficial, and community-driven Windows client for the Fluxer network. Enjoy a native app experience, smart caching, and seamless background updates.

Download for Windows View Source

Optimized Performance

Built with Electron, FluxCap offers smart background refreshing and cache management to keep your memory usage low.

Auto-Updates

Never worry about downloading new versions. FluxCap securely checks for updates and installs them automatically.

Native Experience

Enjoy a system tray icon, custom window framing, and secure handling of external links straight to your default browser.
