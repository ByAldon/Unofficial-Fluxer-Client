FluxCap & Server Stability

FluxCap is an unofficial, community-driven client. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by the official Fluxer application.

Fluxer is a relatively new service and their servers may occasionally experience downtime. If the client fails to load or becomes unresponsive, it is usually due to Fluxer's servers and not a bug.

If this happens, right-click the app in your system tray (bottom right) and select "Clear Cache & Reload", or click the broken wifi icon on the bottom right of the client.

Please do not report network or server-related issues to the FluxCap GitHub repository.
